To play FizzBuzz, input a number. FizzBuzz will count from 1 to the number you input and display the results. Every number divisible by 3 will be replaced with display "Fizz." Every number divisible by 5 will be replaced with display "Buzz." Every number divisible by both three and five will be replaced with display, "FizzBuzz." If the number is not divisible by 3 or 5 it will appear unchanged.
